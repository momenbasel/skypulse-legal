# Privacy Policy for SkyPulse - Flight Tracker

**Last Updated: March 2, 2026**

## Overview

SkyPulse ("we", "our", or "the app") is a flight tracking application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Information We Collect

### Flight Data
- Flight numbers you manually enter for tracking purposes
- This data is stored locally on your device

### Subscription Data
- If you subscribe to SkyPulse Pro, Apple handles all payment processing through the App Store
- We do not collect, store, or have access to your payment information

### No Personal Data Collection
- We do not require account creation
- We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers
- We do not track your location
- We do not use analytics or third-party tracking services

## Data Storage

All flight data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not transmit your personal data to external servers.

## Flight Information

The app retrieves publicly available flight status information (departure/arrival times, gate numbers, delays) from third-party aviation data providers. This information is not personally identifiable.

## Third-Party Services

- **Apple App Store**: Handles subscription purchases and management
- **Aviation Data Providers**: Provide publicly available flight status information

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
